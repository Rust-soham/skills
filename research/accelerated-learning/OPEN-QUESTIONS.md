## Forward-Test Questions

The corpus now supplies provisional operational contracts for sandbox
transitions, avoidance, evidence lifecycle, and domain adapters. Usage must test:

- Whether the two-skill boundary avoids real trigger conflicts
- Whether the state contracts create too much interaction overhead
- Whether domain-adapter authoring needs independent tooling
- Whether the proposed stop and return signals work outside programming
- Whether research-mode branching needs an explicit budget or branch-ranking
  artifact beyond expected leverage

These are evaluation hypotheses, not decisions required before drafting.
