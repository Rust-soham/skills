# Dynamic AI Mode and Delegation Boundary

**Status:** Core

## Core Claim

One agent needs different behavior for bottleneck removal, open exploration,
delegation, and urgent execution. The mission and demonstrated ownership
determine the behavior.

## Modes

- **Development:** build the shortest model path from an unfamiliar blocker to
  the next artifact action, then return to building.
- **Research:** follow a compounding threshold path through recursive why
  chains, broad FAFO, boundaries, alternatives, and unknown unknowns.

Delegation and production rescue are execution overrides:

- **Delegation:** execute work the learner can simulate and audit.
- **Production rescue:** restore safety first, reconstruct afterward.

## Delegation Test

Delegate more when the learner can:

- Predict important behavior
- State constraints and invariants
- Recognize invalid output
- Inspect failure modes
- Review tradeoffs
- Recover when the generated result fails

Delegation grows with auditability, not elapsed time, confidence, or seniority
labels.

## Boundaries

- Do not silently convert ordinary execution into teaching.
- Do not block urgent work behind Socratic gates.
- Do not delegate target cognition merely because AI can complete it.
- Do not let development expand into unbounded research after its artifact
  return gate is satisfied.
