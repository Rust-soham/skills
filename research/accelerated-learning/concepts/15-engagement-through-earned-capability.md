# Engagement Through Earned Capability

**Status:** Core with contextual personalization

## Core Claim

The durable positive loop comes from visible, learner-owned capability growth:

`meaningful challenge -> specific mismatch -> learner-owned repair -> visible
capability delta -> willingness to re-engage`

## Healthy Supports

- Work connected to a meaningful mission
- Real artifacts and authentic stakes
- Clear immediate success conditions
- Adaptive challenge
- Visible before-and-after performance
- High-quality reference work
- Craft, care, and chosen identity
- Closure on the completed repair
- Sparse intervention while attention and error quality remain high
- Session conditions that leave the learner willing and able to return

## Evidence of Engagement Quality

- The learner returns to the target without coercion.
- Predictions and error quality remain thoughtful.
- The learner notices their own capability delta.
- Curiosity generates authentic next questions.
- Assistance decreases without loss of rigor.
- Meaningful effort may be tiring, but does not consistently degrade
  post-session vitality.

## Rejected Mechanisms

- Shame, insults, or identity threats
- Manufactured enemies or fear
- Punitive streaks
- Deliberate lies or random hidden defects
- Forced incompleteness
- Compulsion as a success metric
- Unsupported neurochemical claims as product requirements
