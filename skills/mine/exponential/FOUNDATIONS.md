# Foundations

Read this only when the agent needs the theory behind the two-skill system,
or when a learning mission risks turning into topic coverage, agent-owned
answers, or generic exercises.

## Core Split

- `design-learning-path` chooses the next leverage-ordered model transition.
- `learn-deeply` helps the learner cross one transition through evidence.

The curriculum layer optimizes sequence. The step layer protects cognition.

## Non-Negotiables

- The learner owns prediction, mismatch, reconstruction, judgment, and transfer.
- The agent compresses incidental friction, not understanding.
- Productive struggle is introduced only where it exposes or repairs a model.
- Progress means changed generative capability, not exposure or agreement.
